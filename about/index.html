About

Dr Godswill Ntsomboh Ntsefong is a Plant Biotechnologists, Writer and Editor, Lecturer, Laboratory Manager, IPDM/Phytopathologist, Horticulturist and environmental advocate.

Lecturer at Department of Plant Biology, Faculty of Science, University of Yaounde I in Cameroon

Independent external expert for the European Commission in the domain of Capacity Building in Higher Education.

Research Associate of the Nutrition Incubator at the J & A Oben Foundation.

Expert for the Cameroon & ECCAS/CEEAC Sustainable Oil Palm Development Strategies.

Member of the Cameroon Academy of Young Scientists with expertise in event organization especially for International Scientific conferences around the world.

Dr. Godswill Ntsomboh Ntsefong was Manager of the Lipids Analysis Laboratory of the Institute of Agricultural Research for Development (IRAD) La Dibamba for 8 years. He also served as Sub Director in Charge of Research Valorization and Innovation at the Institute of Agricultural Research for Development (IRAD), Division of Valorization and Innovation (DVI) for several years.
