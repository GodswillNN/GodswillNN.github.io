  • Dr. Godswill Ntsomboh Ntsefong is a highly accomplished plant biotechnologist and geneticist with a specialization in crop improvement. He currently holds the position of Senior Lecturer at the University of Yaounde I. He is also a visiting lecturer in several state and private universities. Dr. Ntsefong obtained his PhD, which focused on oil palm fatty acid genetics, demonstrating his expertise in this specific area of research.
  • Prior to his current position, Dr. Ntsefong served as the Sub-Director at IRAD (Institute of Agricultural Research for Development), where he oversaw research valorization. He has also held leadership roles in laboratories and seed production units, indicating his proficiency in managing scientific facilities and coordinating seed production efforts.
    • With a strong commitment to advancing scientific knowledge, Dr. Ntsefong has an extensive publication record. He has authored over 40 articles, contributed to book chapters, and published a book, showcasing his contributions to the field of plant biotechnology and genetics.
  • Dr. Ntsefong's expertise extends beyond academia. He is actively involved in consulting work, providing valuable insights and guidance in his areas of specialization. Additionally, he takes a leadership role in agribusiness and eco-consciousness initiatives, showcasing his commitment to sustainable agricultural practices and environmental awareness. Recognized for his expertise, Dr. Ntsefong has been appointed as a European Union independent external expert for higher education capacity building, highlighting his contributions to enhancing educational programs in the field of agriculture. He has also been an EMBO fellow, indicating his participation in prestigious scientific networks and collaborations. Furthermore, he has received training from BecA-ILRI (Biosciences eastern and central Africa - International Livestock Research Institute) in Kenya and Eppendorf, further enhancing his skills and knowledge in laboratory management and equipment operations.
  • Dr. Ntsefong actively promotes science through workshops and conferences, sharing his expertise and insights with fellow researchers and students. His areas of expertise encompass plant biotechnology, genetics, biological agriculture, crop improvement, oil palm research, laboratory management, leadership, and entrepreneurship in agricultural sciences.
  • Overall, Dr. Godswill Ntsomboh Ntsefong is a distinguished scientist and academician who has made significant contributions to the field of plant biotechnology and genetic research. His expertise, leadership abilities, and commitment to sustainable agriculture make him a valuable asset in both academic and practical settings.
