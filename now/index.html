Now

In my current position, activities are geared at:

  • Transfering research results or scientific knowledge and key agricultural innovations to end-users, including farmers, agro-industries, relevant public and private sector personnel, policy makers, etc.;
  • Promoting agribusiness links or partnerships between the public and private sectors for the transfer of agropastoral technologies to enhance agricultural development and growth;
  • Making the general public and end users aware of the availability of the various agricultural innovations developed by the research;
  • Mentoring and supervising younger scientists on ethics and scientific excellence

What is a “now page”?

Most websites have a link that says “about”. It goes to a page that tells you something about the background of this person or business. For short, people just call it an “about page”.

Most websites have a link that says “contact”. It goes to a page that tells you how to contact this person or business. For short, people just call it a “contact page”.

So a website with a link that says “now” goes to a page that tells you what this person is focused on at this point in their life. For short, we call it a “now page”.

See examples by browsing nownownow.com.
